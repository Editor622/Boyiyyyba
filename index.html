About Us

A School Built on
Big Ideas

Boyit Geni School was founded on a simple belief: every child carries the seed of greatness. Our role is to nurture it — through inspired teaching, a rich curriculum, and a culture that celebrates both academic achievement and personal growth.

We serve students from nursery through secondary level, offering a holistic education that balances rigorous academics with arts, sports, and community engagement.
